### Debi Das - SLN2024

Symbio - Diabetes Prevention Personal Companion

What is Symbio?
Symbio is an AI-powered continuous diabetes prevention companion - not a programme with a start and end date, but a 24/7 personalised relationship that combines AI-driven lifestyle guidance with human clinical oversight, adapting to each person's culture, mental health, dietary habits, and life circumstances.

What gap does it address?
The current diabetes prevention system is episodic, generic, and passive. The NHS Diabetes Prevention Programme offers the same twelve-week curriculum to everyone - regardless of cultural food identity, mental health, comorbidities, or motivation profile. It ends at week sixteen and withdraws all support. There is no continuous monitoring, no cultural personalisation, no mental health integration, and no financial incentive for the system to actually prevent the disease rather than just deliver the programme. Over 40% of those referred never start. The majority who do regress within six months of finishing. The cliff edge is structural, not accidental.

Why does it matter?
Type 2 diabetes costs the NHS GBP10.7 billion annually - projected to reach GBP16.9 billion within 25 years. It is largely preventable. The populations most at risk - South Asian communities with two to four times the baseline T2D risk, onset from age 25 - are precisely those the current system was least designed to serve. Every year of delayed prevention compounds both the human cost and the systemic cost. AI makes continuous, personalised prevention economically viable for the first time. The market exists; the tools now exist; what has been missing is a product built around the real person rather than the average patient.

Why is it relevant for LBS?
LBS draws students from over 70 countries. A significant proportion carry elevated genetic risk - South Asian, Middle Eastern, East Asian backgrounds - arriving into an environment that compounds it: high stress, disrupted sleep, alcohol-heavy networking, dietary dislocation, and zero time for group health programmes. They are in their thirties and forties, the exact window where metabolic risk accumulates silently before becoming irreversible. They feel healthy. They are not being reached by any current tool.

Beyond the cohort itself, LBS trains the people who will lead healthcare systems, invest in health AI, design corporate wellness programmes, and set workforce health policy globally. Demonstrating Symbio works across LBS's cultural diversity is not just a pilot - it is the proof-of-concept that unlocks every market that follows.
